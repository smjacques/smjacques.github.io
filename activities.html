Selected Projects

fractaL

The purpose of fractaL is to use data in which the chaos is a key-factor and translate them under two main form – sound and visual projection – using different computing languages to reach a more accurate representation.

LAIAdb

. LAIA is a friend-to-friend network (F2F), which only allows access to the data by trusted nodes on the network. The project aims to improve collaborative research collaboration between groups while protecting both research groups and their data against censorship, persecution and other forms of political retaliation.

Climobike

Climobike is a mobile monitoring system focused on collaborative environmental data sampling. The device is adaptable to different models of bike and is capable to store and share real-time georefered data.

Drink Me

Beba-me (Drink me) is a bilingual text adventure game showing how human activity effects the local water eco-system of hilly region in Brazil. The research elected the environmental impact of the waterways for the game to create more understanding and empathy about the quality and quantity of the water supply.

BALCAR

Long-term research on the emission of greenhouse gases (GHG) by hydroelectric reservoir in different regions of Brazil.

ECOlagoas

Project that took place at the Restiga de Jurubatiba's National Park, a region impacted by oil industries activities. The interdisciplinary of the project was developed with a strong social approach covering the "restinga" ecosystem and the surrounding community.
