Saulo is PhD in ecosystem ecology with experimental research testing the effect of climate changes on the microbial community and the nutrient cycle. Former member of Aquatic Ecology lab of Federal University of Rio de Janeiro and from the Bromeliad Working Group of the University of British Columbia currently is developing free technology projects focused on ecological studies, citizen science, agroecology and technological sovereignty. His areas of interest are climate changes, social ecology, decentralized platforms, sensorial implementation of environmental data, biological diversity, urban ecology, security and physical computing.
